THE

FOURTH DIMENSION

On the 24th of February 2021, THE FOURTH DIMENSION has been founded, a group of producers specialized in the Wave genre. We strive to become better at producing and grow a bigger audience, we do this by helping each other through feedback on our tracks, by promoting one another, or any other way to support the fellow members. We hope you will enjoy the music we will bring out in the near future. <3
